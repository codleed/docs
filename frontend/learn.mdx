## Overview

The learning section is the core of the student experience. It provides access to all curriculum content organized by board, grade, subject, and chapter.

## Page Structure

### Board Selection

Students first select their education board (Federal, Punjab, or Sindh) and grade (9th or 10th).

### Subject Grid

Displays available subjects with icons and chapter counts.

### Chapter List

Lists all published chapters for a subject with:

- Chapter number and title
- Completion status
- Quiz availability indicator

### Chapter Detail

The main learning view with:

- **Summary** — Rich-text chapter overview
- **Exercises** — Step-by-step solutions with difficulty badges
- **Flashcards** — Swipeable term/definition cards
- **Quiz** — MCQ assessment with instant results
- **AI Tutor** — Chat button to discuss chapter topics

## Key Interactions

- Exercise solutions are revealed step-by-step
- Flashcards support keyboard navigation (arrow keys)
- Quiz timer counts down and auto-submits when expired
- AI tutor context is linked to the current chapter
